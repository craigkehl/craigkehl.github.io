Meet Sara

Sara is a mom with three children. She is thirty-two years old and belongs to the middle class. She used to work professionally as an office manager but quit to be a full-time mother when here first child was born. She has always been very active with her co-workers and loves competition. Now she is very active trying to entertain her childern.

Senario #1

Sara's oldest plays soccer. This means she has soccer games every Saturday. The league she plays in is very competitive. Given Sara's own competitiveness, she enjoys the enviroment. However, with her two youngest, she has to be prepared for any type of weather. Sara relys on WeatherOrNot.tv's reports to help her prepare. The fact that she can enter in her daughter's soccer game locations, dates and times to get notifications about the weather during the games makes Sara a devoted user.

Senario #2

Her two youngest are not in school yet but very active. It the weather is good, Sara often takes them to the park. When the weather is not, she enjoys the speacials offered by activity providers, such as the Natural History Museum's special offers advertised on WeatherOrNot.tv's alternative activities suguestions.

Meet Robert

Robert is a busy college student. He has many classes, is constantly studying, and works part-time to offset education costs. He knows he needs to stay active, but only enjoys the outdoors on pleasant days.

Senario #3

Due to Robert's busy schedule, he loves the feature that allows him to put in his schedule and the amount of time of his exercise goals. WeatherOrNot sugguests the best days and activities to meet his preferences.
