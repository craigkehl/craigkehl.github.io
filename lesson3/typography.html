Typography

WeatherOrNot is an active site promoting activity and need to portray that spirit in its biggest headings

The three font families are:


  • 'Trade Winds', cursive - Used for h1 headings
  • 'Righteous', cursive - Used for h2 to h6 headings
  • 'Muli', sans-serif - Used for paragraph copy
