Responders dig out man buried in Hyrum trench collapse

by Steve Kent
The Herald Journal, Feb 17, 2020

A man was injured when a trench he was working in collapsed, burying him up to his neck in Hyrum on Monday afternoon, according to police-radio scanner traffic.

First responders were called out to the scene at 680 E. 100 South shortly after 1 p.m. and had extricated the man by about 1:30 p.m.

Cache County Sheriff’s Office Lt. Mikelshan Bartschi said workers were digging around a sewer line when the trench caved in on one of the workers. Dirt was initially covering parts of his face, and co-workers dug out around the man’s head so he could breathe. More...
