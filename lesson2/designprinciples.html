Design Principles

champion-porsche.com

White Space

Champion Porsche is the highest volume Porsche Dealer in the world. Their product speaks volumes and they recognize that. By allowing plenty of white space around their photos, they allow the user to begin imagining what it would be like to own the car.

www.lamborghinipalmbeach.com

Contrast

Brett David, owner of Prestige Auto in Miami Florida, once told me, "When you want to be somebody you buy a Ferrari. When you are somebody, you drive a Lamborghini." Why do Lamborghini owners drive vibrant and often neon-colors Lamborghinis? Because they want to stand-out. That is what contrast does. It screams, "Hey! Pay attention to me."

www.instagram.com

Visual Hierachy

Instagram, one of the most daily used websites in the world, presents many different options on its site at the same time. Plus, Instagram's users vary greatly in their tecnical ability. Therefore, Instagram masterfully uses Visual Hierachy to guide users to the most popular options. The site seems simple to new users, while experienced users maintain ready access to the many other options in a single view. With so many features presented in this way, novice users comfortably increase thier knowledge of and maintain interest in discovering all the things they can do to capture and share life's worthwhile moments.
